About Us

As My Tutor Jungle's tutors, we teach, rethink and customise our knowledge for each student, as every child is different. We are passionate in transferring new knowledge to students & rethink and customise new ways to motivate individual student.

Driven by our passion and enthusiasm to make a difference, we decided to combine these values and enhance the tutoring industry.

My Tutor Jungle wishes to enhance the tutoring experiences into a convenient, unique and honest way such that parents will have a full experience when hiring a teacher for your child.


What matters most

  • Committed tutors

    • Our tutors make a commitment to My Tutor Jungle & parents on the period they wish to teach.

  • People experience

    • Specific personalities of the students that the tutors have taught before.

  • Endorsement

    • Testimonials given by ex-students or parents are helpful to provide parents the comfort they need when selecting a new teacher.

  • Negotiation

    • Able to negotiate for an affordable tuition rate by using commitment period.
